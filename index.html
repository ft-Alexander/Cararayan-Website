WELCOME TO CARARAYAN

A Great Place to Visit and Discover

Cararayan is a barangay in the city of Naga, in the province of Camarines Sur. Its
population as determined by the 2020 Census was 10,692. This represented 9.41%

SCHOOLS IN CARARAYAN

A Place To Learn And Educate The Youth


Cararayan National Highschool

Cararayan National High School (CNHS) is a public educational institution located in Naga, Camarines Sur. The school provides quality, fair, culture-based and complete basic education to produce graduates who are intellectually and technologically competent.The school implements a K to 12 basic education curriculum that offers junior and senior high school. It is under the resolution ordered by the Department of Education (DepEd). It has GAS under the Academic track as well as Technical Vocational Livelihood (TVL) track which equips the students with technical skills for its senior high school program.

Don Manuel Elementary School

Don Manuel Elementary School (DMES) is a DepEd Managed urban public Elementary School Located in Naga City, Camarines Sur
